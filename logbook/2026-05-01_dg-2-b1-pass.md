The runner validates `sum_j gamma_j E_j^dagger E_j = 0` before evaluating the K comparison. All three fixtures satisfy HPTA as algebraic identities:

- σ_z fixture: `(I - iaσ_z)(I + iaσ_z) - (1+a²)I = 0`
