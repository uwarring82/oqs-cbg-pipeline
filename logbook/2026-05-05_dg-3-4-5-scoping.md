## Summary

This is a **scoping** entry, not a verdict. No DG status transitions to PASS. The repository's DG-3/4/5 rows transition from `NOT YET ATTEMPTED` (effectively un-scoped) to `SCOPED — frozen cards awaiting implementation`, recording that the bounded benchmark targets for each gate are now operationalised in card form.

The intent is the same cards-first / Risk #6 / Risk #8 discipline applied at DG-1 and DG-2: a card freezes the parameters and acceptance criterion *before* the runner extension, the reference-method implementation, or (in DG-5's case) the model API exists. Future implementation work is bounded by the cards' frozen acceptance criteria; post-hoc range tightening is supersedure under `docs/benchmark_protocol.md` §6.3, not silent revision.
